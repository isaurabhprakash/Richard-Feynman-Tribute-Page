Here's a timeline of Richard Feynman's life:

  • 1918 - Richard Phillips Feynman is born in New York CIty.

  • 1939 - Eanrs Bachelor's Degree from MIT.

  • 1942 - Earns Ph.D from Princeton University.

  • 1942 - Mrries highschool sweetheart Arline Greenbaum.

  • 1942 - Joins the Manhattan Project as the leader of the theoretical division.

  • 1945 - Arline Greenbaum dies.

  • 1950 - Remarries Mary Louise Bell.

  • 1956 - Feynman and Bell divorce.

  • 1960 - Feynman remrries again to Gweneth Howarth.

  • 1965 - Wins Nobel Prize in Physics for work in quantum electrodynamics,along with colleagues Julian Schwinger and Shinichiro Tomonaga.

  • 1979 - Received the National Medal of Science award.

  • 1986 - Consulted for the Rogers Commission during the investigation of the Challenger space disaster.

  • 1988 - Feynman passes away at the age of 69 in Los Angeles, California.
